Admin Commands (BruceCooper Only)

!archive
Begin archiving process (must confirm with !confirmarchive).
!confirmarchive
Confirm archive and clear all current votes.
!archivedelete <filename>
Stage a specific archive for deletion (confirm with !confirmdelete).
!archivedelete all
Stage all archives for deletion (confirm with !confirmdeleteall).
!confirmdelete
Confirm deletion of the staged archive.
!confirmdeleteall
Confirm deletion of all archives.
!voteremove all
Stage removal of all votes (confirm with !confirm).
!voteremove last
Remove the most recent vote from any user.
!voteremove <game name>
Remove one vote for a specific game from any user.
!confirm
Confirm pending action (e.g., clearing all votes).
